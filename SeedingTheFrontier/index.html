Seeding The Frontier

In this uniquely surprising and eclectic Art exhibition, we seek to remember groups and individuals who helped new desirable culture emerge on our planet. The launch of this online Exhibition complements the November 4-9, 2024, physical Art Exhibition held by the Arts/Sci Lab at the University of Texas atDallas. Its particular objective was to celebrate the life of Tom Linehan, a hybrid professional and adventurous pioneer in emerging technologies in the arts.

"Seeding the Frontier" was formulated by Roger Malina, Director of the Arts/Sci Lab and a close collaborator with Tom Linehan between 2012 and 2020 when Tom retired from UT Dallas. The Co-curators of this Exhibit are Beverly Kleiber aka Reiser, Eric Fullbright, Andrew Blanton and Christine Malina.

This website is a living archival presence of works of art of a group of artists pioneers whose work deserves to be remembered and built upon by future generations.
